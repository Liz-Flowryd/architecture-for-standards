Architecture for Standards

A navigable map of taxonomies, rules and reference patterns across the asset lifecycle. Use it to locate the standards that apply to your role and the gaps that remain to be closed through industry collaboration.

Liz Towler · Founder, Flowryd · Executive in Residence, Global Digital Finance

Standards in primary markets are distributed across data taxonomies, legal master agreements, regulatory frameworks, operational conventions and technology specifications — owned and maintained by different communities. None is sufficient on its own; none is in conflict with the others. What the industry has lacked is a navigable map showing how they fit together.

The matrix below places the asset lifecycle on one axis (issuance, lifecycle servicing, leverage) and the standards stack on the other (data, legal, regulatory, operational, technology). Click any cell to see the taxonomies, rules and reference workflows that apply at that intersection, with explicit reference to the bodies that maintain them.
