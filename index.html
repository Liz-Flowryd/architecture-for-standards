How calculated
1 Subscriptions / day Investor purchase events. Each generates one primary issuance cycle through FLOW-009 Steps 1–7. Default 100 is illustrative — dependent on fund AUM target and distribution reach.
2 Redemptions / day Investor exit events. Modelled at 2 on-chain steps (registry update + cash release). Typically 30–50% of subscription volume at steady state.
3 Steps per transaction FLOW-009 on-chain Canton transactions per subscription: compliance gate, credential check, NAV lock, token mint, registry update, DvP settlement. Range 4–8 depending on which steps generate discrete Canton ledger commits.
4 Secondary trades / day Peer-to-peer MMF token transfers post-issuance. 1 TPS each (atomic Canton transfer). Volume is a function of fund liquidity profile and secondary market activation.
5 Collateral reuse velocity Number of times each MMF unit is reused as collateral within a day (posted, released, reposted). Applied as a multiplier on secondary trade count. GDF TMMF sandbox Simulation 5 demonstrated 2–3x intraday reuse.
6 Repo settlements / day FLOW-001 intraday repo cycles triggered from FLOW-009 Step 8 (Collateral Agent). Each repo settlement = 8 Canton transactions (FLOW-001 step count). Activated once MMF units enter the collateral pool.
Indicative model only. All inputs require refinement once fund details are confirmed — including AUM target, subscription frequency, distribution partner reach, and collateral eligibility terms. Secondary market figures assume FLOW-001 activation at Step 8.
